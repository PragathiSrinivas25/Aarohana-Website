Our Impactful Projects

Initiatives creating tangible change in communities across Bengaluru

Seeds of Service - A Stationery distribution drive.

The stationery distribution campaign by Aarohana Foundation focused on providing the underprivileged children with stationery supplies to aid their education along with drawing meaningful interactions between the children and the interns of Aarohana.

The event offered the interns an opportunity to learn from grassroots efforts, interact with the children through educational and recreational activities, and gain deeper insight into the values of empathy, leadership, and community development.The project aimed at establishing a deep connection with the children making it an eye-opening experience on how small, kind gestures have a lasting impact.

ReRoad Bengaluru

“Roads should connect lives, not claim them.”On a quiet evening in Bengaluru, a group of determined citizens gathered at Freedom Park with a single mission — to demand safer roads and an end to the menace of potholes.

The initiative, titled REROAD Bengaluru, was not just a protest, but a collective outcry for accountability, responsibility, and change.

Ride Green Day (E-Transport awareness at SJCC)

On 20th August 2025, Aarohana Foundation and the SJCC Eco Club held ‘Ride Green Day’, aligning with SDG 12 - Responsible Consumption and Production. Students were encouraged to use sustainable transport such as electric vehicles and public transport, with 100 badges and certificates awarded to appreciate students to recognize their contribution to reducing pollution and fostering eco-friendly responsibility.

Closing the circuit (An E-Waste collection initiative)

“E-Waste collection Drive is an initiative by Aarohana, through a signed MOU with the SJCC Eco- club and Sahaas Zero Waste as the authorized collector. Three collection boxes were placed on campus to collect electronic waste from students and staff. The inauguration of this ongoing drive has raised awareness about proper e-waste disposal. This initiative is taken with the idea to focus on the importance of individual action in sustainability while reinforcing our commitment to sustainability.

PlanetPreneurs - The Sustainability Challenge

Planet-Preneur was an initiative designed to channel youthful creativity into solving real-world sustainability issues. It provided a space where participants could reimagine solutions to everyday environmental challenges and present them in an accessible, idea-driven format.

The focus was on generating innovative yet practical approaches across areas such as waste management, energy efficiency, eco-friendly technology, and community-based impact projects.

Spreading Warmth and Nourishment: Food & Blanket Drive

In December 2024, the Transformative Youth Community carried out a heartfelt initiative to bring comfort and hope to the streets of central Bengaluru. The drive was focused on reaching the most vulnerable, who were homeless and the differently abled with the essentials of warmth and nourishment during the harsh winter days.
