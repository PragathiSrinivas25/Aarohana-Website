Our Impactful Projects

Initiatives creating tangible change in communities across Bengaluru

Reroad Bengaluru

A silent protest against BBMP's apathy and neglect of Bengaluru's roads. Our pothole-facing initiative focuses on identifying, reporting, and repairing hazardous roads, in partnership with civic bodies and local volunteers.

This initiative has gained significant media attention and community support, demonstrating the power of youth-led action in addressing civic issues.

Education for All

Providing quality education resources to underprivileged children across Bengaluru. Our initiative focuses on setting up learning centers, providing study materials, and connecting volunteers with students who need academic support.

This program has helped hundreds of children continue their education and achieve better academic results.

Green Bengaluru

An environmental initiative focused on increasing the green cover in Bengaluru through tree plantation drives, park maintenance, and awareness campaigns about environmental conservation.

This project has transformed several barren areas into green spaces and raised awareness about environmental issues among city residents.
