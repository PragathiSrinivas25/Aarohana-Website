About Aarohana

Working since 2024 | Established in 2025

Turning Compassion into Collaboration

We are a youth-led organization dedicated to coordinating and delivering comprehensive charitable support across our community. From emergency relief and educational workshops to healthcare outreach and environmental initiatives, we mobilize resources, volunteers and partnerships to address every form of need.

Rooted in the collaborative spirit of our founding youth community, we ensure that every act of service—no matter how large or small—reflects our commitment to compassion, empowerment, and sustainable impact.

Registered under the Karnataka Societies Registration Act, 1960
