Meet Our Team

The passionate individuals driving our mission forward

Our team brings together diverse expertise, innovative ideas, and a shared commitment to creating lasting social impact. We are united by the same goal: to empower communities and build a better future.

Executive Committee

The leadership team guiding our organization

Mohammed Rayeed Zain C A

Founder

Meet Mohammed Rayeed Zain, A graduate from St. Joseph's College of Commerce. His mission is simple yet powerful, to bring people together and extend a helping hand to those in need. In these times, when the threads of community often feel frayed, he aims to weave them back together through acts of kindness, support, and collaboration.

Ankith Pawal Kujur

Co-Founder

Introducing Ankith, a graduate from St. Joseph's College of Commerce. He is deeply inspired by the words of Chris Grosser "Opportunities do not happen. We need to create them.” Ankith is committed to fostering unity, empowerment, and positive change. His passion lies in bringing people together to address local challenges, promote inclusivity, and enhance our collective well-being.

Eman Syed

General Secretary

Inspired by Rumi's words, 'Let the beauty of what you love be what you do.' Eman is a Public Policy and Governance student at Mount Carmel College, Autonomous, who is passionate about bridging societal divides and empowering communities through policy. She believes in creating impact, fostering hope, and driving change. With valuable experience in NGOs, Societies and Media Organisations, she aims to leverage her knowledge and skills to contribute meaningfully and serve with purpose.

Shrihari Srinivas

Joint Secretary

Meet Shrihari, a 2nd year student at St. Joseph’s College of Commerce. Excited to begin his journey with Aarohana Foundation he believes that being part of a cause-driven organization is both a valuable learning opportunity and a chance to contribute meaningfully to society. He looks forward to growing alongside the team and working towards making a positive impact.

Nimisha Maria

Treasurer

As Treasurer and Head of Public Engagement at Arohana Foundation, Nimisha combines financial discipline with a passion for connecting people. With past experience leading relief efforts at an NGO and a deep love for community service, she is committed to driving Arohana’s mission forward through transparency, teamwork, and meaningful outreach.

J K Keno

Executive Member

Meet Keno, a 3rd-year student at St. Joseph’s College of Commerce proud to be stepping in as an Executive Member at Aarohana Foundation. From data crunching to getting hands dirty on the field, he loves being at the frontline—whether it's planning, organizing, or simply making a difference. He believes in growing, giving, and going beyond and is excited to collaborate, learn, and make Aarohana soar even higher.

Andrew Joannes

Treasurer

With an eye for detail and a love for storytelling, he transforms ideas into visually compelling content that captivates and engages. He’s the kind of creative who shows up on time because inspiration waits for no one, and neither do deadlines.
